# research-tiers — research port with confidence-gate escalation

**Status:** designed.

Research is a first-class cross-cutting primitive: a lightweight cited web search by
default; `deep-research` is the escalation tier a confidence gate reaches for — low
confidence on a consequential decision triggers research rather than a coin flip,
making "I don't know" a surfaced state. Rigor scales with consequence.

## Acceptance

- Low confidence on a consequential decision triggers research; rigor scales with
  consequence.
